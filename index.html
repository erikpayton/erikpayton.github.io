Composition
and Writing

I am capable of writing for a multidude of mediums such as film, television, and video games. Within those broad types, I excel at composing music for adventurous plots, dramatic stories, and light comedies.

Pencil & Paper
Analog & Digital

I am comfortable writing with manuscript paper, and with software. Utilizing a mix of synthesizers, sampling, foley, and live recording allows me to create unique and breathtaking scores.

Communication

I have the ability to maintain professional relationships with the people I work with. This allows me to work through problems effectively, which is especially useful around deadlines.

My Work

A small collection of the compositions that best represent my style.

  • The Jungle

    A highly varied composition that explores the use of unconventional arrangement. An adventurous and action heavy theme.

  • Beauty

    The idea behind this piece was to come up with a method of writing beauty into music. I am happy with the result.

  • The Stars

    A whimsicle, mysterious theme based on Bernard Herrmann's soundtrack for Vertigo. I find this style particularly useful for establishing shots.

  • Brickadia

    This is an unofficial theme for a game called Brickadia. By utilizing unusual instrumentation, I was able to create something unique. I had fun writing this quirky and comedic piece.

  • Derelict

    Derelict represents my minimalist style by making use of synthesizers, foley, and sampling to form a texturally complex composition.

  • The Coast Medley

    Story telling is something I do best. This composition emphasizes emotion and thematic transitions.
