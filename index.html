What can I do?

With ten years of writing experience, I have a deep understanding of music theory, composition, and orchestration that allows me to create captivating and emotionally resonant scores.
I also have a keen sense of narrative and understand how music can enhance and support the emotional impact of any subject.

Composition
and Writing

I have a diverse range of skills when it comes to writing different genres as a composer. Whether it's creating epic orchestral music, emotional and intimate music, electronic and experimental music, jazz and world music, or pop and rock music, I'm capable of delivering a score that enhances the story and resonates with the audience.

Pencil & Paper
Analog & Digital

My technical expertise in using music production software and hardware, including DAWs and virtual instruments, enables me to produce high-quality music that exceeds industry standards. I have also developed a strong foundation in writing music with manuscript paper and notation.

Communication

I have the ability to maintain professional relationships with the people I work with. This allows me to work through problems effectively and understand the requirements and expectations of my peers. I have a clear understanding of musical terminology and language, which allows me to articulate my musical ideas in a way that can be easily understood by people from diverse backgrounds and with varying levels of musical knowledge and experience. I am also skilled in managing my time, and understand the importance of meeting deadlines and delivering high-quality work on time.
