Erik Payton

Biography

Erik Payton is a classical and contemporary composer from California. Born into a musical household, Erik has excelled in writing and performing music. He is in the process of recieving a Bachelor of Professional Study from Berklee, in order to have a detailed understanding of music. His current skillsets in film score.

With five years of music writing experience, he has written over eighty original works, many of which have been used in both commercial, and non-commercial settings. In the years that have passed, Erik has become a multi-instrumentalist for piano, guitar, saxophone, and flute. Because he is influenced by numerous modern, classical, romantic, and post romantic composers such as Thomas Newman, Bach, Tchaikovsky, and Debussy, Erik has combined their techniques to write for a wide variety of film and media. Additionally, he has attained song writing skills from collaborative projects with other musicians and film students.
