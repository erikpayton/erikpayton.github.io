Erik Payton

Biography

Erik Payton is a classical and contemporary composer from California. Raised in a musical household, Erik has excelled in composing and orchestrating music. Formally educated by mentors and professors at colleges like Berklee, he has developed a deep understanding of theory and composition.

With six years of music writing experience, he has written numerous pieces of music, many of which have been used in commercial, and non-commercial settings. He specializes in writing with digital tools and notation which enhance his musical storytelling. Additionally, he has attained song writing skills from collaborative projects with other musicians and film makers, giving him the ability to expand to other mediums. His current work involves composing for video games and short films.
